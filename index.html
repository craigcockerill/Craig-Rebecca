A little bit about us.

We met three years ago — at this point we're comfortable admitting that it was… online. While maybe not the most conventional, our relationship never needed to be. We talked online for weeks before finally meeting — we can both recount the evening before we met. Hoping the conversation still exists in person, the nervousness, the waiting. But we also remember how natural the connection felt, and that on our respective drives home we shared the same thought. This was a night that would likely define the rest of our lives.

Since then we've endured countless career changes, a slew of vet visits, four apartments, and one cross country move. After taking an incredible job opportunity, we packed the Corolla with our two cats and as much as we could fit, sold the rest, and headed to California. We first moved to Palo Alto, and more recently moved to Berkeley after Craig took a job opportunity in San Francisco.

For us, our wedding day represents a lot of things. It is a ritual of creating a more formal union between each other and our family, it is a long time coming, it is something that we made our minds up about a long time ago, and it is something that we cannot wait for.

Registry Information

So this is a question that a lot of you have asked — we apologize for not having a direct answer sooner. First off we would like to say thank you if you're even reading this section — your presence is celebration enough, so any gifts are received with incredible gratitude.

In the time period from when we registered to this very moment a lot has transpired in our household. Craig had an accident that landed him crutches, with an impending surgery. Though arguably more tragic, after our move, several pieces of our IKEA furniture have breathed their very last breaths. Word to the wise, never unassemble and then reassemble any IKEA products.

That means in the very near future we're looking at several very large ticket items — a new couch, a new bed, a kitchen table, and coffee table if you were wondering. While vegetable spiralizers, pizza stones & slicers, and cutting through soda cans with ninja knife sets would be great — we have bigger and more immediate needs.

If you'd like to help us with these bigger purchases we'd be endlessly grateful — visa gift cards, cash, whatever you would prefer. But we understand this isn't the approach for everyone, if you would prefer to go the more traditional route you can view our registries at William Sonoma and Bed, Bath, & Beyond.
