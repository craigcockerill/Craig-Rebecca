Our Story

We met three years ago — at this point we're comfortable admitting that it was… online. While maybe not the most conventional, our relationship never needed to be. We talked online for weeks before finally meeting — we can both recount the evening before we met. Hoping the conversation still exists in person, the nervousness, the waiting. But we also remember how natural the connection felt, and that on our respective drives home we shared the same thought that this was a night that would likely define the rest of our lives.

Since then we've gone through countless career changes, a slew of vet visits, four apartments, and one cross country move. After taking an incredible job opportunity, we packed the Corolla with our two cats and as much as we could fit, sold the rest, and headed to California. We first moved to Palo Alto, and more recently moved to Berkeley after Craig took a job opportunity in San Francisco.

For us, our wedding day represents a lot of things. It is a ritual of creating a more formal union between each other and our family, it is a long time coming, it is something that we made our minds up about a long time ago, and it is something that we cannot wait for. Hopefully we'll see you there.
